Privacy Policy — Sequence Trip

Last updated: 7 July 2026

Sequence Trip (package dev.stimmie.oeisvisualizations) is an app for exploring and animating integer sequences from the On-Line Encyclopedia of Integer Sequences (OEIS). This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your data.

Data we collect

None. Sequence Trip has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We cannot identify you.

How the app works

The full sequence dataset ships inside the app as an on-device database. Search and browsing run entirely offline on your device. Nothing about your activity leaves your device for our servers, because we do not operate any.

Network requests

The only network requests the app makes are direct fetches to the public OEIS website (oeis.org) when you open a sequence that needs additional terms (b-files). These requests contain the sequence identifier you are viewing and standard technical information (such as your IP address, which any web request necessarily includes). They are handled by OEIS under their own terms; we neither see nor log them.

Permissions

The app requests only internet access, used solely for the OEIS requests described above.

Children

The app collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date above.
